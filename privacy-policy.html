Privacy Policy

How Signature Trust Insurance collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

Signature Trust Insurance is an independent insurance agency located in Naples, Florida, operated by Bruno Garcia, a licensed Florida insurance agent (License #W861718). Our website is signaturetrustfl.com.

When you contact us or submit a quote request through this website, you are communicating directly with a licensed agent — not a lead aggregator or third-party marketplace.

Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to provide you with an accurate insurance quote or respond to your inquiry. This may include:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • ZIP code or property address (to determine coverage availability in your area)
  • Coverage type of interest (auto, home, flood, commercial)
  • Details you provide specific to your coverage request (e.g., vehicle count, property type, year built)
  • Your preferred contact method and best time to reach you
  • Language preference (English or Spanish)

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or driver's license numbers through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through our quote form is used solely to:

  • Contact you to discuss your insurance needs and provide a quote
  • Follow up on your request via phone, email, WhatsApp, or SMS — based on the contact preference you indicated
  • Maintain a record of our communication for service continuity

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact by Phone, Text, and Email

By submitting a quote request on this website, you consent to be contacted by a licensed Signature Trust Insurance agent regarding your inquiry. This may include calls, SMS/text messages, and email to the contact information you provide.

If you contact us via WhatsApp, we may continue that conversation through WhatsApp as your primary channel. You can opt out of any communication channel at any time by letting us know.

We do not use automated dialers or mass marketing systems. All outreach is personal and conducted by a licensed agent.

Data Storage and Security

Quote request information is stored in our secure customer relationship management (CRM) system, hosted on infrastructure with industry-standard encryption and access controls. Access to this data is limited to licensed agency staff.

We retain your information for as long as necessary to maintain our business relationship or comply with applicable Florida insurance regulations.

Cookies and Analytics

This website may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site. These tools may use cookies — small files stored in your browser — to collect anonymized usage data such as pages visited and approximate geographic region.

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to any legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of future communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly at bruno@signaturetrustfl.com or call (239) 850-2268.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of this website following a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
